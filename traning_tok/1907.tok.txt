AUSTRALIAN WHEAT EXPORTS RISE IN FEBRUARY .
Australian wheat shipments rose to 1.33 mln tonnes in February from 1.01 mln in January , an Australian Wheat Board official said .
February 's shipments were down on the 1.54 mln tonnes shipped in February 1986 .
Cumulative shipments for the first five months of the October/September wheat year were 6.12 mln tonnes , as against 6.54 mln a year earlier , the official said .
