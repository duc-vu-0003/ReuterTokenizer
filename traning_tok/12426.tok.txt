PRICES RISE AT BANGALORE COFFEE AUCTION .
Prices rose at the fortnightly export auction here on March 25 for sales of 1,907.1 tonnes of coffee from the 1986/87 and 1985/86 crops out of a total offering of 1,940.7 tonnes , the Coffee Board said .
Withdrawals amounted to 33.6 tonnes .
