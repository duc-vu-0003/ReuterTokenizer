INDONESIAN WHEAT IMPORTS EXPECTED TO FALL IN 1987 .
Indonesia 's wheat imports are expected to fall to 1.5 mln tonnes in calendar 1987 from 1.69 mln in 1986 , the U.S. Embassy 's annual agriculture report said .
It said the drop was expected , because there will be a drawdown on stocks built up near the end of 1986 .
It said wheat stocks at the end of 1986 were 390,000 tonnes , up from 223,000 at end-1985 .
It forecast end-1987 stocks at around 290,000 tonnes .
