THAI NATURAL RUBBER EXPORTS RISE IN 1986 .
Thai natural rubber exports rose to 763,331 tonnes in 1986 from 689,964 a year earlier , the private Board of Trade said .
Japan , the biggest buyer , imported 384,622 tonnes of Thai rubber in 1986 , up from 348,855 the previous year , it said .
