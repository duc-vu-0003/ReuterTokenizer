PAKISTAN CONSUMER PRICE INDEX FALLS IN JANUARY Pakistan 's consumer price index -LRB- base 1975/76 -RRB- fell to 231.93 in January 1987 from 233.26 in December 1986 , and compared with 223.66 a year ago , the federal Bureau of Statistics said .
The wholesale price index -LRB- same base -RRB- rose to 229.83 in January from 229.06 in December and compared with 217.97 in January 1986 .
