ASCS SEEKS OFFERS TO PROCESS CCC ROUGH RICE .
The Agricultural Stabilization and Conservation Service -LRB- ASCS -RRB- is seeking offers to process rough rice owned by the Commodity Credit Corporation -LRB- CCC -RRB- and deliver about 27.0 mln pounds of milled rice for export shipment May 6-20 and May 21-June 5 , an ASCS spokesman said .
Offers must be received by 1300 CDT April 7 , and successful offerors will be notified April 10 .
